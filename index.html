How it all began...

A quick glance at my life.

I was born 1987 in Austria and had since an early age a fascination with Technology but we hadn't the Money to buy a Computer for me. I re when i got a cheap V-Tech Computer which i took apart to see how it worked.

In Elementary School i was always a good pupil and had good grades. I had my problems with German and Geography but i could overcome those problems. With my Grandpa i did a lot of hiking and crafting, he was a carpenter which made a lot of fun.

With 10 Years old i went to in High School. Because of my laziness i had bad grades and had to switched to Secondary School where my grades greatly improved and i finished the School successfuly.

After i finished Secondary School with 15 i began an Apprenticeship as a Car Body Worker where i also learned the Basics of electronics. Unfortunately i could not finish the Apprenticeship because the Company closed.

After the abortion of my Apprenticeship i took a Year off from Working and started my journey in the Computer Scene. A Friend of mine teached me Linux and Hacking he pushed me in the right direction so i would learn by using google by myself.

I celebrated my first successful WIFI Hacks and continued my journey in Linux Administration in deploying amy own Web Server. I was also very active in the Download SCene at that Time. This continued for a couple of Years until i got into Arduino, Raspberry und other Microcontroller for Home and Hacking Tools.
